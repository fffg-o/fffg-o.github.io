I’ve also looked at several blog websites. They are basically all the same—aside from different CSS, their essence is identical. I don’t understand it, and I don’t like it.

Do all blogs need a comment function? I still prefer the so-called Web1 style. Building a full feature set for a place that basically no one reads or replies to doesn’t feel worthwhile (mainly due to the cost).

So in the end, I still lean more toward creating a personal website—where the homepage isn’t a blog and has no comment function. Maybe in the future I’ll add some strange and interesting features—who knows.
